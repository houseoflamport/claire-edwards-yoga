Pregnancy Yoga

Starting our classes is no different to starting any other form of exercise — the same advice applies. If you are not used to regular exercise, please move carefully and wait until after 15–16 weeks. If you have symptoms, feel unwell, have a temperature, or are in a high risk category, please do not attend in person — participate via Zoom instead. If in doubt, consult your doctor or midwife.
